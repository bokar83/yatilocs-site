Six Ways We Serve Your Crown

Every service is built around the specific needs of Black and African hair. Not generic. Not improvised. Practiced.

01

Loc Creation

Your locs journey starts here. We assess your hair type, discuss your goals, and lay a foundation built to last. No guesswork. No shortcuts.

02

Maintenance and Retwist

Regular maintenance is what separates thriving locs from struggling ones. We keep your roots tight, your edges clean, and your crown healthy.

03

Loc Styling

Barrel rolls, two-strand twists, updos, and beyond. Whether it’s a weekend look or a special occasion, Yati knows how to style for the moment.

Wash and Deep Conditioning

Locs that are clean and hydrated at the root are locs that grow strong. Our wash service goes deep — not just on the surface.

Scalp Detox and Hydration

The scalp is where everything starts. Buildup, dryness, and tension all live there. We address the root — literally — so your locs can thrive.

Beard Grooming and Lining

A strong beard is part of the full picture. Clean lines, shaped edges, and a groomed beard complete the look from crown to chin.
