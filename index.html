Secrets of the Locsmith

Care That Comes From Experience

Proven guidance for nurturing your crown from the inside out. Real artistry begins with real care.

01

Moisture Is the Move

Hydrate your locs regularly with water-based sprays and seal with natural oils like jojoba or castor.

02

Sleep Smart

Wrap your hair at night in a satin or silk scarf to reduce friction and retain moisture while you rest.

03

Clean, But Don’t Strip

Use sulfate-free shampoos that cleanse without drying. One to two washes per week is often ideal.

04

Hands Off

Avoid excessive twisting or manipulation. Tension at the roots and edges adds up over time.

05

Respect the Journey

Locs evolve over time. Trust the process, stay consistent, and embrace every phase of growth.
